Hello,all! I hope you all must be tensed about the biggest engineering exam of our country and also the most awaited event of the year JEE Main 2019.
We all know the exam is going to be fully online this year,this has its benefit as well as losses but we should never ever worry about it . We should be well prepared and try to give our best .
As this is the last week of the countdown ,what are the things we should do and we should consider to emerge as a champion on the d-day.
Regarding increasing score in actual paper,here are some points,which are experiences of your seniors ....do read them for a blissful JEE.

Solve indiviual papers with a set time limit for physics and mathematics,as much as you can..this is something very productive and this can increase your score drastically .Solving 3-4 papers daily will benefit you most.
Do not turn pages from your theory,focus on paper solving at this stage..
Try to cover complete all the important topics from chemistry..
If you have not done any particular mains topic,do not panic .....leave it..
For problems in mathematics ,i will suggest you to first think and then approach a particular problem..
Keep revising your short notes..
Do not study any new thing at this stage.Remember every single question paper you solve will increase your mark by 3.
Do not eat much,not less.... eat in a balanced way.Beware of constipation
Do not try a new posture while sleeping ,sleep comfortably .I had a severe pain in my back on the d-day..
Try to sleep before 9:00 ,because it is going to be the most difficult sleep for you,it won't come easy..
Check the transportation facility and your arrangement to your centre,one of my friend nearly missed his exam due to this problem.
Keep all your documents with you and check yourself before going..
Keep yourself away from diseases at this crucial time.
Please revise the important formulaes from solid states,trigonometry,centre of mass,RBD,indefinite integral,optics ,statistcs etc.These are very important.
